Axure RP License FAQs

Can I use Axure RP on more than one computer?

Each subscription can be used by the user on any number of computers including across PCs and Macs.

How can I upgrade from RP 9 and below?

If you are a current subscriber just download the latest version and sign in. For license key holders for RP 9 and below, RP 10 is only available as a monthly or annual subscription. Please enter your email address (or the license key buyer’s email) in the Cart or sign in to the License Portal to purchase your upgrade to Axure RP 10. Learn more about what’s new in Axure RP 10 here. Please contact sales@axure.com with any questions.

How can I upgrade editions?

You can upgrade from Pro to Team at any time. The remaining value of your subscription will be applied to the upgrade cost.

Do you offer a discount to any organizations?

We offer a 60% discount to nonprofits and educational institutions, as well as a 25% discount to government agencies. To request a quote, contact sales@axure.com.

Where is your End User License Agreement?

Axure RP is sold as a licensed product according to the terms and conditions of the Customer Agreement.

Do you offer Perpetual Licenses?

Axure no longer offers perpetual licenses. Monthly and annual subscriptions are available. Your existing perpetual licenses will not be affected.
